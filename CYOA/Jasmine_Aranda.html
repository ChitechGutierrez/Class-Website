John F. Kennedy - November 22, 1963

A Choose Your Own Adventure Story Game.

By Jasmine Aranda

John F. Kennedy was the 35th President of the United States, but his journey ended early due to his assassination that occurred on November 22,1963. He accomplished many things and if he weren't assassinated, he could have accomplished more, including the things he didn't get to finish.

Are you ready to play?

It’s November 22, 1963. You’re in Dallas, Texas guarding the motorcade that John F. Kennedy is attending. You’re standing nearby with the other guards, your job is to keep watch of everything, make sure there is no conflict and that everyone is safe. As you’re standing around, one of the guards tell you that they have a feeling that something is going to go wrong. He tells you about how me noticed something suspicious coming from the sixth floor of a nearby building. You think that it might be nothing, but there’s still a chance that something could go wrong. Do you go and check out the building?

You brush off what your friend told you and decide to continue to guard the motorcade. As you’re watching the motorcade, you hear a gunshot followed by the sound of everyone screaming. There’s people running, screaming and pushing. There’s people getting shoved to the ground being continuously stepped on. You notice that the sound of the gunshot came from the building your friend was suspicious about. What do you do next?

As you continue to guard the motorcade you hear a gunshot. Everyone is running around screaming, there are people getting pushed and stepped on. You push through the crowd trying to get to who ever was shot. Once you get there, you see John F. Kennedy in the car bleeding. He gets rushed to the hospital and you later on find out that he died due to his injury. The next day after John F. Kennedy’s death, Lyndon Baines Johnson takes over as president. During his presidency he tried to do his best to lead in the way that John F. Kennedy would have done.
