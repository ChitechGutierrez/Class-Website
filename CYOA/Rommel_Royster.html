Later on, Rommel rose and got win after win and made hitler's job that much more easier . Hitler recognized this and soon after that he became hitler’s right hand man. Hitler sadly was no fool, He founded out that rommel would try to overthrow him ,his mood quickly changed and in the process noticed something else that he never thought of as a possibility. The only person who could do the job was rommel because he has never lost a battle and he’s strong and he actually treats his soldiers like something other than pawns. So he thought why not eliminate the problem, He gave rommel a choice to commit suicide and spare his family lives or don’t be killed and they die with him . Him being noble he spared his family an commited suicide.

Rommel turned down the offer and continued to work under hitler and he gave up those who was planning to betray him. Rommel called a meeting with the un-loyal soldiers and he lead them to a room.
