Time is Precious

A Choose Your Own Adventure

By Leron Lacy

You are a time traveling shapeshifter, against government regulations you travel back in time to prevent some of the world's greatest tragedies. It pains you to see people get hurt and with the current world we live in you think it's time for a change. You decide to take on the roles of different figures of power during World War II. But it's not that easy the G.T.T ( government time travelers ) are hunting you down in an attempt to stop any of your time rifts( drastic changes in history that have immediate effects)with the motive to kill if necessary. You have the choice of matching history as much as possible with minor changes or to use your own choices to prevent the war causing multiple time rifts.

Are you up to the challenge?

The year is 1939 and you pose as Neville Chamberlain, british prime minister. Pre-war tensions are very high and you have the choice to sign the mutual assistance treaty with poland, essentially stating that they will help protect the independence of polish citizens in the event of war or invasion. You slip up before you make your decision causing a time anomaly( an in consiatancy in recorded events), the G.T.T are close on your tail and you must act quickly before they catch you.

In the spur of the moment you signed the treaty and escaped the G.T.T with the help of a young boy you meet on the street , three days later germany takes action and invades poland this could disastrous hundreds of thousands of lives could be lost. Do you declare war on germany keeping your pact with Poland or do you betray poland.

You declared war on germany and bring all of your allies into the war, world war II breaks out.The G.T.T find you, because of a slip up made while leaving the war room in london, and are on your tail forcing you into hiding until you find the next major event. For three years you hide letting thousands die in the war. You ponder on the idea that you caused the war in the first place and your actions make no difference. The year is 1941 and you pose Franklin D. Roosevelt.You have the choice to place and embargo on japan and freeze its american assets or ignore their plan to invade china. This this is the moment you have been waiting for do you place the embargo and save chinese lives or do you let them be and refuse to see the threat that they pose to the pacific.
