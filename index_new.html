MMAII

MultiMedia Arts II (MMAII) is a course that focuses on a variety of digital media, design principles and technological literacy. We will be learning the Principles of Design, Color Theory and other tools of Graphic Design. We will learn how to use image and video editing software, and a few other tools for creating high quality work. We will also work in the field of Web Design, working with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and jQuery, in order to create high quality websites. We also will spend time learning how to responsibly use technology, as well as learning new things about the current state of tech.

Senior Capstone

In the ChiTech Senior Capstone students are tasked with creating and starting a business within a small group. Based on student passion and prerequisite coursework, students are placed into one of three disciplines and enrolled in the corresponding course: Business Startups, Graphic and Web Design, or Computer Programming. Students from each course form interdisciplinary teams ensuring that each has members with the necessary skillsets. Through a variety of creative thinking and problem solving exercises, teams focus on one single, viable business idea. From there, students are driven to solve problems and meet the needs of the business as they arise. The Design Students, taught by myself, work with the User Experience (UX) approach to design and create product layouts and user interfaces. Business students utilize the Lean Startup methodology of validated learning and iterative experimentation. Programming students work with the Ruby on Rails framework to develop a functioning pre-Alpha level prototype built on the work of the Design students.

This class is cotaught with Jon Bull (Business) and Kevin Musiorski (Programming).
