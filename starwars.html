PART 1: CHAPTER 3
By George Orwell


It is a period of constant war. Enemy countries were attacking the country of OCEANIA, lead by the evil PARTY.

During the battles, people in Oceania go about their day like nothing is wrong. None of them have ever seen the war, but they were told it was happening by BIG BROTHER, so it must.

Tired of the way he has to live his life under the everpresent watchful eye of BIG BROTHER, our hero WINSTON SMITH raced home, custodian of a illegal diary he had purchased for himself. With this, he commited one of the worst crimes of all in Oceania: THOUGHTCRIME.
