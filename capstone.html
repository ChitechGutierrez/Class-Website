ChiTech Senior Capstone Nick Gutierrez, Jon Bull, Kevin Musiorski

In the ChiTech Senior Capstone students are tasked with creating and starting a business within a small group. Based on student passion and prerequisite coursework, students are placed into one of three courses: Business Startups, Graphic and Web Design, or Computer Programming. Students from each course form interdisciplinary teams ensuring that each has members with the necessary skillsets. Through a variety of creative thinking and problem solving exercises, teams focus on one single, viable business idea. The Design Students, taught by myself, work with the User Experience (UX) approach to design and create product layouts and user interfaces. Business students utilize the Lean Startup methodology of validated learning and iterative experimentation. Programming students work with the Ruby on Rails framework to develop a functioning pre-Alpha level working prototype.

This class is cotaught with Jon Bull (Business) and Kevin Musiorski (Programming).
