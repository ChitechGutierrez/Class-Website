Senior Capstone - Design

Mr. Gutierrez's class

Date

September 9th

Agenda

Students will present their high level idea, in exactly 10 words.
Students will then break into pairs. This pair will be part of the same group for the rest of the school year. How they will break up: Good Question.
Pairs will work together, and pick the idea they want to pursue. This idea does NOT have to be one of their originals. They will start high level, and then become more specific. They need to develop a 30 second elevator pitch that they will give on Friday to pairs in other classes.

September 4th

Agenda

Students will complete a skills survey, and pick the class they are most interested, as well as explaining why.

Students will participate in a "Brain Walking" activity. On a piece of paper, along the top, write down a problem. Then, in rotation, pass the paper to the person next to you until you get your original back. Each person writes a possible solution, or expands on something already written.

September 2nd

Agenda

All Call. All students will meet in the Cafeteria for rundown of the classes.

Problem Activity - Think of a problem you face regularly. Write it on a post it and give it to the teacher.
